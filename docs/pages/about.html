About

Marco Saravia is a visual artist and photographer who lives and works in Quito, Ecuador. His work has been exhibited in countries such as Ecuador, Argentina, Uruguay, Brazil, Mexico, and the United States.

He has participated in solo and group exhibitions in spaces such as: Cantor Art Gallery, College of the Holy Cross, Worcester, Massachusetts – USA (January 24 – April 14, 2017). Espacio de Arte Contemporáneo, Montevideo – Uruguay, Season 18 (June 3 – August 16, 2015). Centro Cultural Malvinas, La Plata, Buenos Aires, Argentina (June 8 – 23, 2013). Centro Cultural Leopoldo Marechal, La Plata, Buenos Aires, Argentina (August 9 – October 25, 2012). Posada de las Artes Kingman, Quito, Ecuador (February 20 – 27, 2008) / (August 23 – September 5, 2007). Alliance Française, Quito, Ecuador (July 10 – 17, 2007).

He was awarded the "Senescyt" Scholarship, Universities of Excellence Program 2014–2016. Selected for the International University Visual Arts Biennial, Sixth Edition. Category: "Painting". Autonomous University of the State of Mexico, Toluca, Mexico (November 8, 2013 – January 30, 2014). Selected for the University Art Biennial, First Edition. Category: "Drawing". State University of Minas Gerais, Belo Horizonte – Brazil (November 8 – December 2, 2012).

He holds a Master's degree from Untref, National University of Tres de Febrero, in Technology and Aesthetics of Electronic Arts, Buenos Aires, Argentina (2016–2017). And a Master's degree from UNA, National University of the Arts, in Combined Artistic Languages, Buenos Aires, Argentina (2013–2014).
